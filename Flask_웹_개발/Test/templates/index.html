This is the web page for the patrol car project.


Patrol vehicle robots fuse lidar sensor and camera sensors to perform autonomous driving, and have the following functions.


  1. Identify and control speeding vehicles.
  2. Recognize the accident site and take pictures and action.
  3. Recognize the defective lane and take a picture to show the location.
